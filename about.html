Welcome to our Website

We are a collective that especially deals in exclusively the flyest shoes in the market. Whatever you may want we may probably have it! And even if we don't we probably have something better :|.

This collective, PlugMeup, was founded by Adriel and Fernando. Two innovative Kenyan fashion designers with just but a dollar and a dream. Pulling their resources together they started a shoe shop which grew to what our collective now is. Standing at 20 shops both locally and internationally. These very shops are stocked with shoes handpicked by trusted shoe enthusiasts along with Adriel and fernando's own designs.

2024 marks the thirteenth anniversary of PlugMeup.
